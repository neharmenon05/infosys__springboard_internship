About PolicyNav

PolicyNav is an AI-powered platform to explore and understand Indian government schemes. It combines NLP semantic search, AI summarization (via Ollama), and Quantum predictions to give actionable insights on policies.

Tech Stack

  • Backend: FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Pydantic
  • Frontend: TailwindCSS, Plotly.js, WordCloud2.js
  • NLP: Sentence Transformers, Ollama (local inference)
  • Quantum Module: Qiskit, PennyLane (QCNN/QSVC predictions)
  • Database: SQLite (dev), Postgres (prod)

Mission

Make government policies transparent, accessible, and searchable with AI-powered insights.
